Episode 1: Outcasts
Fira, the last known fire element, groups up with some friends to kick some evil butt.

Episode 2: Discovery
The Elementals return home after a long battle with Zap. Merina's dad grounds her for running away and Airabis makes some unusual discoveries.

Episode 3: Grounded
Lani tries to sneak out but, is caught by her sister. Merina finds a clue about her power gem and Fira searches for a base for the elementals.

Episode 4: A Bad Surprise
It is Lani's birthday and everyone is celebrating, but Zap has other plans.

Episode 5: Sidekicks
A multi-element girl, Windswept, shows up and wants to helo the elementals, but they are not sure they can trust her. Lani discovers that her sister was hidding a clue to her power gem.

Episode 6: At Odds
Lani and Airabis won't get along. Their competitiveness allows Zap to gain information of the location of Fira's power gem.

Episode 7: Crystal's Problem
Crystal's parents come to visit but the elementals soon realize they have a bad relationship with her.

Episode 8: Swapped
As a plan to trick the elementals, Zap uses a spell to switch the girls' elemental powers. They must learn to control their new powers to fight Zap.

Episode 9: No Turning Back
Airabis discovers her power gem, an amethyst, is hidden in the Cliffs of Advinon. Fira must search the ruins of the lost fire kingdom for her power gem, a ruby. "Sidekick" shows up again and the others have to deal with her.

Episode 10: Treatury
Merina searches for her power gem in the great abyss, a dangerous and toxic underwater canyon. Lani and her sister compete for her gem. Airabis finds her power gem and a horrible secret. She must find Fira before it is too late.

Episode 11: Defeat
Airabis and Fira are stuck in Batolm. They must compete for Fira's power gem against Zap. The others ahve claimed their power gems and must fight Scarlet.

Episode 12: Rise
Fira escapes from Scarlet with the help of her anscestor, Asseriya, but she must free Airabis. The elementals must might against Zap. The power gems may be the only thing able to free Zap.
